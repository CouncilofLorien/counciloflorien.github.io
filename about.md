### About

The ***Council of Lórien*** is the official U.S. sanctioning body for the _Middle-earth collectible card game_. The Council of Lórien was operated by Iron Crown Enterprises between 1996 and 1999. It is now the operated by the U.S. MECCG player group.

The ***Counsel of Lórien*** is the official U.S. archivist for the _Middle-earth collectible card game_.
